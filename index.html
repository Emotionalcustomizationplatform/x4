Legal & Privacy

1. Non-Clinical Service: Private Counsel is an emotional support and consulting service. We are not licensed psychologists or psychiatrists. We do not provide medical diagnoses or prescriptions.

2. Emergency Protocol: If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency or suicidal thoughts, please contact your local emergency services (911 in the US) immediately. We are not an emergency response service.

3. Data Privacy: We adhere to a strict "Zero-Record" policy for session content. We do not record video or audio calls. Your contact information is encrypted and used solely for scheduling and billing purposes.

4. Payments: All services are private-pay. We do not accept insurance. Refunds are processed on a case-by-case basis within 24 hours of booking.
